About Me

Hello World!

I'm a junior at Harvey Mudd College double majoring in Computer Science and Visual Art. I'm really passionate about the intersection of technology and design! Although I do have some experience with front-end web development, I'm currently interested in exploring computer graphics and animation. I'm from the Bay Area, but I'll be in Los Angeles this summer for a software engineering internship with Electronic Arts DICE . Hope you enjoy my website!
