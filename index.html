I'm on a life-long journey of learning and growth, and I'm excited to see where I'll go!

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area, where I swam competitively for 10 years and fell in love with learning. From 2016 to 2020 I was in the Los Angeles area while attending Harvey Mudd College, where I double majored and earned a Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science and Visual Art. Currently, I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student with a focus on Computer Graphics at Simon Fraser University in the Vancouver area. Within graphics, I am presently most interested in physically-based animation and geometric modeling. I'm constantly amazed by how math, science, and art come together in order to represent the world around us in a meaningful way.
