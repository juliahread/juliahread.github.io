About

Hello World!

I'm a senior at Harvey Mudd College double majoring in Computer Science and Art (Concentration: Digital Art), and I'm really passionate about the intersection of software, physics, math, art, and design! The most amazing things can happen with software as the medium, physics as the connection to reality, math as a mode of numerical communication, art as the evoker of emotion, and design to tie it all together. Although I do have some web development experience, I'm currently interested in exploring computer graphics and animation. I've also taken a particular interest in procedural generation. I'm Bay Area born, but most recently I was in Los Angeles for a software engineering internship with DICE LA (Electronic Arts).
